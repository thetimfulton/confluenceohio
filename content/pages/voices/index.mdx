## Introduction

Renaming a city is a big question. It deserves a real conversation — not a shouting match, not a social media pile-on, but a genuine exchange of perspectives.

This section is that conversation. We publish voices from all sides: people who support renaming, people who oppose it, and people who are still thinking it through. Submissions are reviewed for respectfulness, not agreement. If you have a thoughtful perspective — wherever you stand — we want to hear it.

> "I get the argument. I really do. But I've been a Columbusite for 40 years. The name means something to me that has nothing to do with the explorer. I'm not ready to give it up."
