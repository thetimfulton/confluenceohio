## Why Volunteer?

This campaign runs on people. Every petition that gets signed, every conversation that gets started, every story that gets shared — it happens because someone showed up. We are building a team of volunteers across Columbus, and there is a role for everyone.

---

## Roles

**Signature Collector**
Gather petition signatures at community events, farmers markets, and door-to-door. We provide training, materials, and support. This is the most direct path to 22,000.
*Time: 2–5 hours/week · In-person*

**Social Amplifier**
Share campaign content on your social networks. Engage in online conversations. Help our message reach beyond the people already paying attention.
*Time: 1–2 hours/week · Online*

**Event Organizer**
Plan and run community events — house parties, public forums, neighborhood meetups. Bring the conversation to your part of the city.
*Time: 3–5 hours/week · In-person*

**Story Collector**
Interview community members and help them share their perspectives for our Voices section. Especially needed: perspectives from neighborhoods and communities we have not yet reached.
*Time: 2–4 hours/week · In-person + writing*

**Neighborhood Captain**
Coordinate campaign activity in your neighborhood. Be the point person for your area — track signatures, organize local events, connect with community leaders.
*Time: 3–5 hours/week · In-person + coordination*

**Design & Content Creator**
Help produce graphics, writing, video, and other campaign materials. If you have creative skills, we have creative needs.
*Time: Flexible · Remote*
