## Key Facts (At-a-Glance)

- **Campaign:** Confluence Ohio — a citizen petition to rename Columbus, Ohio
- **Organization:** 501(c)(4) civic organization
- **Proposed name:** Confluence — the geographic term for the meeting of the Scioto and Olentangy rivers in downtown Columbus
- **Legal mechanism:** Citizen-initiated charter amendment under Section 42 of the Columbus City Charter
- **Threshold:** ~22,000 valid signatures (10% of last general municipal election turnout)
- **Vote required:** Simple majority on the ballot
- **Signatures collected:** [X] of 22,000
- **Founded:** 2026
- **Website:** confluenceohio.org
