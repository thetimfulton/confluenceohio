## Why Donate?

Running a petition campaign takes resources: printing flyers, training volunteers, running digital outreach, hosting community events, covering legal costs, and keeping the lights on. Confluence Ohio is a 501(c)(4) civic organization funded entirely by individual donations. We do not take corporate money.

---

## How Your Money Is Used

**$5** = Prints 25 petition flyers
**$10** = Provides materials for one volunteer shift
**$25** = Funds one community event
**$50** = One week of targeted digital outreach
**$100** = Covers legal filing costs for one month

We publish quarterly transparency reports showing exactly how funds are used. You can find them on our blog.
